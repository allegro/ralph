Ralph

Ralph is an asset management system for your data center.
It lets you see what hardware and software is installed. It also lets you keep track of who is using every device, what is it used for and how much it costs.

Read more

Ralph was initially created in the Allegro Group for internal use. See what it does already and what is planned for the nearest future.
